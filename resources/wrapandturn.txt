Wrap and Turn

Knit Side:
Bring yarn to front between needles
Slip next St on L needle to R needle, purlwise
Bring yarn to back between needles
Slip St from R needle back to L. Trun.

Purl Side:
Keeping yarn in front
Slip next St on L needle to R needle, purlwise
Bring yarn to back between needles
Slip St from R needle back to L
