Adding Stitch Kit to your home screen

Adding Stitch Kit to your phone's home screen will make it easier to bring it up in the future without having to remember a URL. Follow the screenshots below for either iOS (iPhone and iPad) or Android.

iOS

For iOS you should first bring up the application in the Safari web browser. Do not use any other browser.

Step 1: Tap the Share button

Step 2: Tap Add to Home Screen

Step 3: Tap Add

Step 4: Launch using the icon

Android

For Android you should first bring up the application in the Chrome web browser. You may use Firefox, but Chrome is recommended. Do not use any other browser.

Step 1: Tap the Menu button

Step 2: Tap Add to Home Screen

Step 3: Tap Add

Step 4: Launch using the icon
